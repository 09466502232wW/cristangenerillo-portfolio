Project Details

Meow Flap

Meow Flap is a 2D game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird. In this game, instead of a bird, you control an adorable cat. The gameplay mechanics are similar to Flappy Bird, where you tap to make the cat flap its wings and navigate through a series of pipes. Your objective is to keep the cat in the air and avoid hitting the pipes, aiming for the highest score possible. The charming cat character and simple yet addictive gameplay make Meow Flap a fun and engaging experience for players of all ages.

In designing Meow Flap, we focused on creating a visually appealing and user-friendly experience. The game features a minimalist 2D art style with vibrant colors and smooth animations that bring the cat character to life. The interface is clean and intuitive, allowing players to jump right into the game without any distractions. As a UI and UX designer, I ensured that every element on the screen serves a purpose, making the gameplay experience seamless and enjoyable. Meow Flap’s straightforward design and easy-to-use controls make it accessible for players of all skill levels, while still offering a challenging experience that encourages repeated play.
